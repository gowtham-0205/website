Cyber Essentials

Welcome to our Cyber Essentials Workshop designed to provide you with essential skills and knowledge to navigate the digital landscape securely.

About the Workshop

In today's interconnected world, cyber security is of paramount importance. Our workshop will cover a range of topics, from understanding common cyber threats to implementing effective security measures. Whether you're new to the field or looking to enhance your existing knowledge, this workshop is for you.

Workshop Details

  • Date: September 10, 2023
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM (GMT)
  • Duration: 1 day
  • Location: Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Chennai Campus

Agenda

Over the course of one day, you will learn:

  • Introduction to Cyber Security
  • Types of Cyber Attacks
  • Network Security
  • Encryption and Decryption

About Session

Our workshop sessions are designed to be interactive and hands-on. You'll have the opportunity to work on real-world scenarios and learn from experienced professionals in the field. By the end of the workshop, you'll be equipped with practical skills that you can apply to enhance your personal and professional security.

Project Submission

As part of the workshop, you'll be required to complete a practical project that demonstrates your understanding of key concepts. The project submission deadline is September 30, 2023. This project will be an opportunity for you to showcase your skills and creativity in solving real-world security challenges.

Requirements

  • Basic understanding of computer systems
  • Interest in cyber security

Certificate

Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate of participation.
