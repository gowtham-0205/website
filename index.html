Amrita School of Engineering conducts workshops for school teachers, school students, engineering graduates, and engineering college faculty members in emerging trends in engineering and sciences. All the workshops are conducted by the departments/centers under the School of Engineering at various campuses. While some workshops are offered for free, some are charged nominal fees.

Our college values workshops as a means to foster practical skills, creativity, and innovation. We believe in providing diverse learning opportunities that align with the ever-changing demands of the industry.
